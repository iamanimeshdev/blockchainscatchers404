About Vitcoin

Vitcoin is VIT's exclusive cryptocurrency built on the Solana blockchain, enabling students to participate in a decentralized marketplace with fast, low-cost transactions.

Features of Vitcoin

Fast Transactions

Experience lightning-fast transactions on the Solana blockchain.

Low Fees

Enjoy ultra-low transaction costs, making Vitcoin accessible for all.

Highly Secure

Built with the latest blockchain technology to ensure security and trust.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create your account in just a few steps and get started with Vitcoin.

2. Get Vitcoins

Purchase Vitcoins or earn them by participating in campus activities.

3. Use in Apps

Explore various apps like rentals, assignments, and gaming, and use your Vitcoins.
